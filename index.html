What is GAL Group?

GAL Group stands for Genuine Adventurous Ladies. We are an organization of women who want to meet other like minded women while learning practical life skills and gaining new hobbies.

What type of activities do the GALs do?

We plan activities which help Squads work toward various badges. We aim to provide knowledge to women in practical subject areas which they may have never been given the opportunity to learn. The badge topics range from survival skills, to autobody work, to gardening. There are set guidelines for each badge which must be completed to be verified in the given subject. In addition to events directly related to obtaining badges, GAL Squads are encouraged to meet as often as they wish in a social environment! After all, a main purpose of the organization to meet and develop friendships with like minded women.

Can anyone join?

GAL Group is currently limited to women ages 25-40. During our startup we want to do everything we can to gather women who are likely to be in similar places in their lives to encourage lasting friendships. This will definitely be open to change as the group grows and more women become interested. For now - if you identify as a woman and are 25-40 years old, you are welcome to join a local GAL Squad!

How do you join?

You can either join an existing local GAL Squad, or start a new Squad if you have a minimum of four GALs.

Do you need to obtain the Badges in a set order?

Not at all! There are 30+ badges preset for your Squad to pick from at any given time. GAL Group has a select "Badge of the Month" that we recommend based on popularity, season, etc. If you choose to work on this badge during it's selected month, the organization will do it's best to help you plan associated activites. However, a Squad can choose to obtain the badges in any order.

What if our Squad wants to obtain a Badge in a topic not yet created?

We are open to hearing all recommendations for new Badges! If a Squad has an idea for a new Badge that their GALs are interested in obtaining, we would love to help make this happen. The Squad leader just needs to fill out the "Suggest a Badge" form and wait for approval.
